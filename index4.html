Legend Bracelet

Story and Mission

In 2016, Kevin McCroary traveled to the Philippines to serve underprivileged children. It was an experience that allowed him to personally witness the polluted conditions that these children had been born into. This journey allowed him to truly see the impact humanity can have on the earth. After studying the effect of climate change on the environment and started planning a trip to visit the Arctic to witness the effect of climate change.

He arrived in the Arctic after a recent tsunami and he met a group of refugees who lost their homes to the tsunami. One of the refugees, Remy, kept a daily journal of the weather conditions for the last 35 years and noticed that the temperature was getting warmer as a result of the changing climate. This trip solidified that climate change was a reality to people living in the Arctic and the world.

After he returned, it was clear to him that something had to be done and he decide to work on something that will a positive impact on the planet. He wanted to create a brand that will encourage people to "follow your legend",meaning to follow the path your heart is calling to take and leave a legacy on the planet. He decide to launch "Legend Bracelet".

The organisation's mission is to educate people about climate change through a consumer product and introduce them to issues such as thawing permafrost and scientific organizations conducting research to help protect the environment.

Bracelets and Partnerships

The bracelet has two capsules; the first capsule is filled with sand or other materials that have been collected from the ground or sea. The second capsule is left empty, meant for you to fill with sand/earth from someplace special to you. soem capsules are filled with materials that have been collected by scientists from the Woods Hole Research Center, in which they study the carbon content in permafrost. Permafrost is the frozen ground that is thawing and releasing carbon emissions in the atmosphere.

12% of the net earnings will be donated to organisation that are protecting the climate and helping climate refugees. Those earnings will we spread equally amongst the organiations that Legend Bracelet has partnerd with.

After a purchase of a bracelet, the funds go to the charities that they represent: the gorilla bracelet is representing the Gorilla Doctors, who are working to save Gorillas through veterinary care, the arctic bracelet represents the Woods Hole Research Centre, which researches the carbon emissions from thawning and working to fight climate change, the coral bracelet represents the Mote Marine Laboratory, which focuses on restoring coral reefs, the elephant bracelet represents the Reteti Elephant Sanctuary, who are saving orphaned and abondoned elephant calves and releasing them into the wild, the sea turtle bracelet represents the Olive Ridley Project, who are saving injured turtles from plastic pollution and the koala bracelet represents the Adelaide Koala Rescue, that helps to rescue and rehabilitate koalas and releasing them to the wild.
