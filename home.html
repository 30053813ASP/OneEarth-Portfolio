Through pollution, we are releasing carbon dioxide from our vehicles and in to the atmoshpere, which causes the global temparature of the air and ocean to rise. We are also releasing plastic waste into the ocean, causing many marine wildlife to be destroyed. Many scientist from the world agree that we are the primary cause of global warming and climate change.
